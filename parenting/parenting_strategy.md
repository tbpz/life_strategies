# Parenting Strategy — Son (Born 2025)

**Goal:** Raise a son who is physically healthy, emotionally secure, socially competent, and knows how to learn — across all dimensions of growth, not just academics.

**Parenting model:** Authoritative — high standards + high warmth. Authority earned through fairness, not demanded through position.

**Current state:** Son is 9 months old (May 2026). Both parents aligned on all principles below.

---

## 5 Core Principles

Decision filters — when unsure about a parenting choice, check here. → Full detail: [`reference/01_parenting_principles.md`](reference/01_parenting_principles.md)

| # | Principle | One-liner |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Authority Through Reasoning** | Every rule has an explainable reason. "Because I said so" is the emergency brake, not the steering wheel. |
| 2 | **Trust Without Collapse** | Close enough to share true thoughts. Distant enough that authority is real. Never punish honesty. |
| 3 | **Teach the Engine, Not the Fuel** | How to learn > what to know. Praise effort and strategy, never innate talent. |
| 4 | **Independence as the End Goal** | Every decision passes one test: does this move the child closer to self-reliance? |
| 5 | **Family as Foundation, Not Cage** | A place to return to, not escape from. Closeness through genuine connection, not obligation or guilt. |

## 6 Development Domains — FOMO Checklist

If all 6 are addressed, nothing critical is being missed. → FOMO evidence & research: [`reference/02_fomo_and_evidence.md`](reference/02_fomo_and_evidence.md)

| Domain | What It Covers |
|---|---|
| 🧠 **Cognitive / Learning** | Problem-solving, curiosity, metacognition, how-to-learn |
| 🗣️ **Language / Communication** | Talking, listening, reading, vocabulary, expression |
| ❤️ **Social-Emotional** | Emotional regulation, empathy, relationships, self-control, identity |
| 🏃 **Physical / Motor** | Gross motor, fine motor, physical activity, body awareness |
| 🏥 **Health & Nutrition** | Sleep, screen time, nutrition, medical care, safety |
| ⚖️ **Moral / Character** | Right vs. wrong, values, ethical reasoning, responsibility |

> **Key insight:** These develop together through the same activities — one floor play session covers Physical, Cognitive, Language, Social-Emotional, and Health simultaneously.
