## Core Identity & Values
*Non-negotiable principles that drive decisions.*

- **Purpose & Meaning:** Work and pursuits must feel genuinely meaningful. Won't chase goals (titles, salary, status) that don't connect to a personal "why."
- **Autonomy:** Self-directed. Rejects blind authority and decisions without rational justification.
- **Intrinsic Joy:** Energy comes from the process itself — constant small wins and tangible progress. Financial outcomes are secondary; the work must be enjoyable.
- **Competence & Mastery:** Deep need for genuine capability and recognition for actual skill, especially in high-confidence areas.
- **Authenticity:** All major decisions must originate from genuine personal desire, not social expectations, fear, or external validation.
- **Rational Adaptability:** Commits based on best available reasoning; pivots without guilt when real-world experience reveals misalignment. No sunk-cost attachment.
